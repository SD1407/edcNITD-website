    About E-Summit

    Our flagship event, E-Summit, currently in its 4th edition, has hosted some of the most prominent stalwarts of the industry over the years and is famed as the second largest E-Summit of its kind in eastern India. Through a gamut of events such as entrepreneurial guest talks, workshops, competitions and investor meet-ups, E-Summit aims to help students get exposure about entrepreneurship and build a strong start-up ecosystem in the country.
    This year, E-Summit NIT Durgapur comes to you from the comfort of your homes on 2nd, 3rd and 4th April 2021.

    Events

    During E-Summit, we host a series of entrepreneurial events. Workshops, competitions, discussion panels, business pitches and E-Talks are conducted. The main objective behind these events is to cultivate entrepreneurial qualities in students and motivate them to dream big.

    Auction Arcadia

    Auction Arcadia brings an opportunity to bid for the players and form your IPL teams. A dedicated auctioneer controls the proceedings of this event and at last, it ends with a settles squad for every IPL Team. With online game simulation, E-SUMMIT ’21 brings this unpredictable game into play by organising Auction Arcadia.

    E-Talks

    There is no better learning about entrepreneurship than hearing it from the people who have carved an undisputed name in their respective fields. At E-Talks which draws in huge crowds every year, we will bring in some of the finest and most distinguished names from a range of diverse fields. From YouTube superstars to CEOs and Founders, we have it all covered.

    Pitchfest

    This E-Summit’21, PITCHFEST brings you the opportunity to present your B-Plan in front of eminent personalities in the world of business and get mentored in the right direction. Winners might even get a chance to be funded by our investors

    Past Speakers

    Ms. Prajakta Koli

    YouTuber, popularly known as “MostlySane”

    One of India’s biggest YouTube stars, Prajakta Koli, spoke at E-SUMMIT’19 to a jam-packed audience of over 6000 students. She covered a range of topics, from battling stereotypes about women in comedy to her perspective on the reason behind her insane fame.

    Mr. Rahul Kaushik

    Founder, The Melting Words

    Mr. Rahul Kaushik’s work and his page, The Melting Words has been wildly popular among the youth and the enthusiasm of the crowd was certainly unparalleled. The session included topics ranging from the source of inspiration behind his works to his formula for mega social media popularity.

    Ms. Shaan Suhas Kumar

    Miss India Earth 2017

    The maiden edition of E-Summit in 2018 proudly hosted the very talented, Ms. Shaan Suhas Kumar, India’s representative at the Miss Earth 2017. She spoke about her incredible journey of being a girl from a small city in India to achieving international fame by representing India at one of the biggest beauty pageants in the world.

    Mr. Mir Afsar Ali & Mr. Indrajit Lahiri

    (Indian RJ, TV Anchor) & (YouTuber, Blogger)

    During the virtual E-Summit 2021, we had with us, Mr. Mir Afsar Ali, the MIRacle himself, and Mr. Indrajit Lahiri, or FOODKA. Through the session, they enlightened us on the thought behind their initiative FOODKA, and of course we gathered some insights on how comic relief can bring us out of the gloomiest of situations

    Mr. Abhiraj Rajadhyaksha and Ms. Niyati Mavinkurve

    YouTuber Couple, popularly known as Abhi and Niyu

    Abhi and Niyu, immensely popular content creators who together gained popularity through their initiative #100ReasonsToLoveIndia spoke at the virtual E-Summit 2020. In the panel discussion, the duo revealed that one of the reasons behind their #100ReasonsToLoveIndia series was to educate millennials about the beauty of India and the historical significance it holds.
